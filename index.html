Hi there, I'm Soon Hing

Nice to meet you, I am a Robotics and Software Engineer with a strong passion for technology and innovation.

In 2010, I was inspired by the movie "Iron Man 2" and embarked on my robotics journey. I started learning how to craft my IoT stuff, and diving into programming to make them come alive.

Projects

Throughout my journeys, I have been building and playing around with different robots. After every project, I am getting closer to build my own iron man suit.

Smart Robot Car

This is a RaspberryPi powered robot car, programmable to perform image classifications and navigate itself around obstacles.

Robust Control for Robot Arm

Utilizes force torque sensor to perform precise task like peg-in-hole assembly. It is able to compensate for unprecise initial insert, and uses a robust control system to strategically locate and insert pin into hole

Ella, The Robot Barista

6 DOF robotic arm manipulation and path planning to perform pick-and-place task to serve cafe latte to customer.

Image Classification in Scratch3

I created this extension in NodeJS for Scratch3, it has a very user-friendly interface, and it is perfect for people who has no prior experience to machine learning and AI to try to create their own model for image classifications.

IoT Home - Light Switch

Connecting a NodeMCU to Blynk, I achieved a simple remote controllable light switch. I can remotely turn on and off the light using my phone or laptop.

Cup detection with brand recognition

A coffee vending machine that is smart! It is able to scan if the user places an actual cup. On top of that, it gives discount if the user decides to be environment friendly and bring their own cup.

Tennis Collector

An academic competition. Two robot cars will start in the arena at the same time. Whoever collect the most ball without falling out the arena win the game. There were 8 teams and we ended up the 4th place.
